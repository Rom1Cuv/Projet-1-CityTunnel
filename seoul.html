Martial Arts

Let's talk about their martial arts, which is Taekwondo! Taekwondo is a Korean form of martial arts characterized by punching and kicking techniques, with emphasis on head-height kicks, spinning jump kicks, and fast kicking techniques. It's a combat sport that's been developed during the 1940's and 1950's. In 2018, the South Korean government officially designated Taekwondo as Korea's national martial art. Yay!

Korean Cuisine

Have you tried Korean Cuisine? If not, then you should try it because it's daebak! (Korean word for saying when something is so great !!) Its largely based on rice, vegetables, seafood and meats. Talking about meat, Korean BBQ is not to be missed! But when we say Korean Cuisine, we can't forget about Kimchi! The most popular and traditional side dish of salted and fermented vegetables.
